What is ProdHacks?

ProdHacks is a hackathon with an emphasis on the end product. We want to help you design solutions to problems facing today’s society. We’ll provide world-class mentorship from industry experts so that you can walk away with valuable insight and an impactful, purposeful project they can be proud of.

What makes ProdHacks Different?

This isn't your typical hackathon — after 24 hours of hacking and working with mentors, you will pitch to judges and be evaluated not solely on coding, but on your product's go-to-market strategy and product synergy as well. ProdHacks revolves around entrepreneurship: a combination of software development, product management, branding and marketing, and business analysis. It’s important to us that teams are diverse in their skill set, so registration is open to any student from any university, program, and level of hackathon/programming experience.

Who can participate?

University students are eligible to attend ProdHacks.

We will keep you fueled throughout the weekend with meals, snacks and drinks.
